THE BEST MOVIE I WOULD SUGGEST

My top 3 movies of all-time

Parasite (2019)

A really interesting movie that talks about rich and poor people and why it matters.

The Social Network (2010)

A cool movie about how Facebook started and how it changed things.

Whiplash (2014)

A movie that shows how hard work and never giving up can help you become great at something, like playing music.
